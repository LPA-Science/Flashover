Dataset title:
Flashover Voltage Dataset for Polymeric Insulators under Environmental Conditions

Version:
1.0

Year:
2026

Dataset description:
The Excel workbook includes separate sheets for individual samples and a final sheet named Whole samples. The Whole samples sheet contains the complete dataset, combining all four polymeric insulator samples, and should be used as the main dataset sheet.
This dataset contains flashover voltage records for polymeric insulator samples obtained through laboratory experiments conducted in a controlled environmental chamber under humidity, ageing, and pollution-related conditions. 
The dataset includes four polymeric insulator samples. 
Each record contains geometry-related variables, relative humidity, accelerated ageing, non-uniform pollution degree, occupation ratio, salt deposit density, and the corresponding flashover voltage.

Dataset size:
6,656 records (experiments)
10 columns

README_FOV dataset.txt

Column summary:
Polymeric Insulator Sample Number: Identifier of the polymeric insulator sample.
Creepage Distance (CD) (cm): Total creepage (leakage distance) along the insulator surface between the two end-fittings of a polymeric insulators.
Arcing Distance (AD) (cm): Shortest air-gap distance between the two end-fittings of a polymeric insulator.
Creepage factor (CD/AD): Ratio of creepage distance to arcing distance.
Relative Humidity (%): Relative humidity condition.
Accelerated Aging (day): Accelerated ageing duration or ageing level.
J (non-uniformity Pollution Degree): Non-uniformity pollution degree of fan-shaped non-uniform pollution.
K (Occupation Ratio) (%): Occupation ratio associated with fan-shaped non-uniform pollution.
Salt Deposit Density (SDD) (mg/cm^2): Salt deposit density representing pollution severity.
Flashover Voltage (kV): Flashover voltage corresponding to the given conditions.

Recommended short column names:
CD, AD, CF, RH, Aging, J, K, SDD, FOV
